Privacy Policy for NickiFy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NickiFy ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

NickiFy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not store the text you enter into the app
  • All text transformations happen locally on your device
  • We do not track your app usage or behavior
  • We do not collect device identifiers or location data

2. How the App Works

NickiFy is a simple text transformation tool that:

  • Takes text input from you
  • Transforms it into stylized Unicode characters
  • Displays the results for you to copy
  • All processing happens entirely on your device

3. Data Storage

The app does not store any data persistently. When you enter text, it is only temporarily held in your device's memory while being processed and is not saved anywhere.

4. Third-Party Services

NickiFy does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. We do not share any information with third parties because we don't collect any information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13, as we do not collect any personal information from anyone.

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data, there are no data security concerns related to personal information storage or transmission.
